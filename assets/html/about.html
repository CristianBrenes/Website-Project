About us
Prime Greenery is a local, family-owned and operated business. My passion for maintaining my own lawn has inspired me to assist others in achieving the same level of perfection. We recognize that not everyone can consistently dedicate time to maintaining their yard, and that's where we step in. Effective yard maintenance significantly enhances curb appeal, and our goal is to ensure your property always looks clean and fresh by staying on top of it.
